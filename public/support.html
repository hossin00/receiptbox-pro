ReceiptBox Pro Help Center

Will I lose my data if I clear my browser?

Data is stored in browser local storage. Export or backup your data regularly using the app's export feature.

Does this app work offline?

Yes — ReceiptBox Pro works 100% offline. No internet connection required (except optional AI features).

Is my data private?

Completely. Your data never leaves your device. We have no servers, no accounts, no tracking.

How do I backup my data?

Use the export feature in the app to download your data. Keep regular backups to avoid data loss.
